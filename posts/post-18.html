In 2022, researchers developed a revolutionary reinforcement learning system that can learn to control chemical processes autonomously. üß† The Reinforcement Learning Solution

Reinforcement learning is a type of artificial intelligence that learns through trial and error, just like humans do. The AI agent takes actions, receives feedback about how well those actions worked, and then adjusts its strategy to improve performance over time.

üåü The Learning Control Framework

The researchers developed a sophisticated reinforcement learning system that can learn to control chemical processes by interacting with them directly. Think of it like having a super-intelligent operator who never gets tired, never makes the same mistake twice, and continuously learns how to do the job better.
