🌱 The Environmental Challenge

Environmental pollution poses significant threats to ecosystems, human health, and the planet's sustainability. Traditional pollution control methods often have limitations in effectiveness, efficiency, or environmental impact. Modern environmental engineering offers new approaches to address these challenges.

📊 The Pollution Problem

Environmental pollution affects air, water, and soil quality, threatening biodiversity, human health, and ecosystem services. Many pollutants are persistent, toxic, or difficult to remove using conventional methods.

⏱️ The Treatment Challenge

Traditional pollution control methods may be energy-intensive, produce secondary waste, or have limited effectiveness against certain pollutants. This creates challenges for sustainable environmental protection.
