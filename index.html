About ChemDecoded

Bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and high school education

Our Mission

I believe that the latest chemistry research shouldn't be locked behind complex jargon and paywalls. As a high school student passionate about chemistry, I work to translate groundbreaking discoveries into stories that inspire other students like me.

About the Creator

Hi! I'm Aundrea Gadsby, a high school student from New Jersey with a deep passion for chemistry. I created this website and write all the blog posts myself. My goal is to make cutting-edge chemistry research accessible to fellow students who are curious about science but might find academic papers intimidating.

What I Do

  • Summarize recent research papers from top chemistry journals
  • Explain complex concepts using everyday analogies
  • Connect research to real-world applications
  • Publish new content every two weeks
